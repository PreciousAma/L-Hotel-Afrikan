Outdoor Pool

We offer all the essential facilities needed to make your stay both convenient and comfortable. An swimming pool for you to cool off after a day of venturing Children under 14 years old must be accompanied by an adult. Parents/legal guardians are fully responsible for their children.

Chaffeur Service

L’ Hotel Afrikan is easy to get to from Copenhagen Airport in Kastrup.You have several options for easy transport. If you have a meeting to catch up with too, the chaffeur service has you covered. you can also request a ride from the airport to any location before you arrive at the hotel. All would be factored into your bill.
